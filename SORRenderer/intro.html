Instructions:

1. Click "Create New SOR"
2. Left click in the canvas to start a line
3. Continue to left click to add as many points as you'd like.
4. Right click on your final point to terminate your line, it will now be rotated about the y-axis and turned into an object.
Now you can use WASD to move the camera and the arrow keys to turn.
You can render multiple objects but they can't move currently so you need to move between them to see multiple.
